Account Deletion Request

This page allows users to request deletion of their account for Spindle Fidget, developed by Xceed.

🔐 What data do we collect?

  • Email address – used for account creation and login.

🗑️ What happens when you request deletion?

  • Your email and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our servers.
  • No data is retained after deletion. We do not keep backups of deleted user data.
  • We will process your request within 7 business days.

📩 How to request account deletion?

Please email us at commerce.saurav@gmail.com using the same email linked to your account. Include a short note requesting deletion.

🕒 Data Retention Policy

We do not retain any personal data after deletion. Once the request is processed, your data is permanently removed with no recovery option.
